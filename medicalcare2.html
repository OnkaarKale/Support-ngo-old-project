Doctors For You (DFY)


Founded in 2007, Doctors For You (DFY) is a team of healthcare professionals and volunteers who provide medical care to communities in crisis, especially during natural disasters and emergencies. DFY has worked extensively during disasters, like the Kerala floods and the COVID-19 pandemic, providing immediate relief and establishing long-term healthcare facilities for affected communities. They also offer free medical services to impoverished urban populations, set up health camps, and run awareness programs on sanitation and hygiene. DFY partners with government bodies and international NGOs to enhance disaster preparedness and ensure healthcare reaches those in need.

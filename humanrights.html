Human Rights

The Human Rights Law Network (HRLN):- is a collective of lawyers and activists dedicated to protecting and promoting human rights in India. They provide free legal aid to marginalized communities and work on issues ranging from gender rights to environmental justice.

Amnesty International India:- Amnesty International India is a branch of the global human rights organization, dedicated to advocating for justice, equality, and freedom from human rights abuses across India. They work to support victims, lobby for legislative change, and educate the public on pressing human rights issues.

Oxfam India:- Oxfam India is an NGO working to tackle poverty and injustice through a human rights-based approach. They focus on empowering marginalized communities, advocating for gender equality, and addressing social and economic inequalities.

PUCL ( Peoples Union for Civil Liberties):- PUCL is a human rights organization working to uphold civil liberties and democratic rights in India. They are dedicated to defending the freedom of speech, protecting minorities, and holding the government accountable.

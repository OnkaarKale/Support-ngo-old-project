Animal welfare

People for Animals (PFA):- is India's largest animal welfare organization, working to rescue, rehabilitate, and protect animals in need. It operates numerous shelters and initiatives across India, advocating for animal rights and supporting animal rescue operations.

Blue Cross of India:- Blue Cross of India, established in 1959, is a trailblazing animal welfare organization that focuses on the rescue, rehabilitation, and humane treatment of animals, particularly stray and abandoned animals. Through their active programs, they champion compassionate care and adoption to improve animal welfare in India.

Wildlife SOS:- Wildlife SOS is a conservation-focused NGO working to protect and rescue endangered and abused wildlife throughout India. Known for their pioneering efforts in saving "dancing" bears, they now work across multiple wildlife rescue and anti-poaching projects.

Animal Aid Unlimited:- Animal Aid Unlimited is a nonprofit organization dedicated to rescuing and rehabilitating street animals in Udaipur, Rajasthan. Through round-the-clock emergency response and community-driven initiatives, they provide critical care to injured and neglected animals.

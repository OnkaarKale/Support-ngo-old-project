Save the Children India


Save the Children India has been active for over 100 years, working to uplift the lives of vulnerable children through education, healthcare, and protection. Their programs reach millions of children across India, ensuring they have access to safe learning environments, nutrition, and medical care. Save the Children also responds to natural disasters and emergencies, providing essential relief and rehabilitating affected communities. The organization works with government bodies and communities to develop child-centric policies, improve healthcare facilities, and raise awareness about child trafficking, child labor, and child rights.

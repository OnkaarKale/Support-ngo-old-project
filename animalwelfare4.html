Animal Aid Unlimited


Founded in 2002 by Jim, Erika, and Claire Abrams, Animal Aid Unlimited is an inspiring example of community-based animal rescue and rehabilitation. The organization operates from Udaipur, responding to emergency calls about injured or sick street animals, including dogs, cows, donkeys, and cats. They have a specialized rescue team and a veterinary hospital with state-of-the-art facilities to treat animals suffering from accidents, disease, and malnutrition.

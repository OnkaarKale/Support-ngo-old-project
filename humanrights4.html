PUCL ( Peoples Union for Civil Liberties)


The People’s Union for Civil Liberties (PUCL) was founded in 1976 by the renowned lawyer Jayaprakash Narayan. It is one of India’s oldest civil liberties organizations, primarily working to protect the democratic and civil rights of individuals against state oppression. PUCL advocates for the freedom of speech, fair treatment of prisoners, and the protection of religious and ethnic minorities. Through fact-finding missions, PUCL documents cases of abuse by state agencies, like police brutality and unlawful detentions. They also conduct public awareness programs to educate people about their constitutional rights and engage in legal advocacy to ensure the rule of law.

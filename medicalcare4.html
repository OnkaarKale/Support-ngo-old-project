The Akshaya Patra Foundation


The Akshaya Patra Foundation, initially focused on feeding children through its mid-day meal scheme, has extended its support to healthcare for school children. Their healthcare initiatives include routine health check-ups, screenings for common ailments, and providing corrective treatment as needed. Akshaya Patra also educates children on personal hygiene, preventive healthcare, and nutrition to support their overall well-being. Working in partnership with government agencies and private organizations, they ensure that children from low-income families receive essential healthcare and grow up healthy, aiding their educational journey.

Child Welfare

Save the Children India:- Save the Children India is a leading NGO that provides emergency relief and long-term support for underprivileged children, focusing on education, health, and protection.


CRY (Child Rights and You):- CRY is one of India’s largest NGOs focused on protecting the rights of underprivileged children. They work to ensure children’s access to education, healthcare, and protection from exploitation


Bachpan Bachao Andolan (BBA):- BBA is a grassroots organization founded by Kailash Satyarthi, focused on eradicating child labor and trafficking and ensuring children’s rights to a safe and healthy childhood.


Pratham:- Pratham is one of India’s largest NGOs focused on improving education quality for children. Known for its innovative teaching methods, Pratham aims to ensure every child can read and learn effectively.

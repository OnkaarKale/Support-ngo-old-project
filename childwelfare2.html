CRY (Child Rights and You)


Founded in 1979, Child Rights and You (CRY) is dedicated to improving the lives of children in India. CRY works with local organizations to address the root causes of issues such as child labor, lack of education, and child marriage. They advocate for children’s rights at multiple levels and implement projects to ensure access to quality education, health, and nutrition. CRY’s initiatives focus on empowering communities to prevent child exploitation and promote child welfare. By working with governments and communities, they have successfully transformed the lives of thousands of children, helping them break out of the cycle of poverty and gain access to a brighter future.

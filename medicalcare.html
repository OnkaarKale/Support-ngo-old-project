Medical Care

Smile Foundation:- is an Indian NGO that focuses on improving healthcare access for underprivileged communities. They provide medical care through mobile health clinics, health camps, and awareness programs, aiming to bridge the healthcare gap in rural and urban slum areas.

Doctors For You (DFY):- Doctors For You (DFY) is an Indian medical relief organization that responds to health crises, such as natural disasters, and focuses on healthcare access for marginalized populations.

Karuna Trust:- Karuna Trust is an NGO committed to bringing primary healthcare to rural and tribal communities, focusing on areas where medical infrastructure is severely lacking.

The Akshaya Patra Foundation:- Akshaya Patra, primarily known for its mid-day meal program, also addresses healthcare needs through its school health programs, providing medical check-ups and treatments for children from low-income families.

Blue Cross of India


The Blue Cross of India, headquartered in Chennai, is one of Indiaâ€™s oldest and most respected animal welfare organizations. Founded by Captain Sundaram, his family, and a few animal lovers, Blue Cross has made significant strides in improving the lives of stray animals across India. The organization runs numerous initiatives, including animal rescue, adoption drives, spay/neuter programs, and anti-cruelty campaigns. Their Animal Birth Control (ABC) program has been instrumental in controlling the stray population, preventing disease, and reducing animal suffering. Blue Cross also partners with schools and communities to promote kindness toward animals, teach responsible pet ownership, and advocate for vegetarian and vegan lifestyles.

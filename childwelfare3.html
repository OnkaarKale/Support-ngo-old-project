Bachpan Bachao Andolan (BBA)


Founded in 1980 by Nobel Laureate Kailash Satyarthi, Bachpan Bachao Andolan (BBA) is one of India’s leading movements dedicated to ending child labor, trafficking, and exploitation. BBA actively rescues children from bonded labor and trafficking, rehabilitating them and reuniting them with their families when possible. The organization also advocates for stronger legislation against child exploitation, conducting awareness campaigns and collaborating with local communities and schools to promote education over labor. BBA’s comprehensive programs focus on protecting children’s rights, providing education, and offering skill development for rescued children, enabling them to lead safe and productive lives.

Pratham


Established in 1994, Pratham focuses on providing quality education to children in both urban slums and rural areas across India. The organization has pioneered methods like the “Read India” program, which uses interactive and easily accessible teaching techniques to help children attain basic literacy and numeracy skills. Pratham also assesses learning outcomes through the Annual Status of Education Report (ASER), bringing critical insights into India’s educational challenges. Pratham works with governments, parents, and communities to improve the standard of education and provide vocational training for youth, bridging the gap between formal education and employment opportunities.
